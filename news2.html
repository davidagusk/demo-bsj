BSJ Students Shine at ReEarth International Art Prize 2025

We are proud to share that BSJ secondary students achieved outstanding success at the ReEarth International Art Prize this year. Year 11 student Chiara was awarded Second Place for her AI generative artwork, Tsunami of Waste—a powerful visual reflection on waste and our responsibility to care for the planet.

We also celebrate further student recognition in the competition: Kirtini (Year 11) received an Honourable Mention for her digital artwork, Harmony in Motion, and Isabelle received a Spotlight Selection Award for her acrylic painting on canvas, Loss of Symbiotic Algae.

At BSJ, the Arts programme supports students to build technical skill, develop a distinctive creative voice, and engage with meaningful themes through research-led making. These achievements reflect not only talent, but also sustained practice, refinement, and purposeful communication through visual language.
