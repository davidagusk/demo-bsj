Open Day at BSJ: Meet Our Teachers & Explore the Campus

We welcome families to our Open Day experience—an opportunity to meet our teachers, tour our facilities, and learn how BSJ supports every student to thrive academically and personally. You’ll gain insight into our approach to learning, wellbeing, and the co-curricular experiences that shape confident, capable learners.

During your visit, you can explore learning spaces and specialist facilities, hear about our curriculum pathways from Early Years through to Secondary and Sixth Form, and speak with our admissions team about the application process and start dates.

Our Open Day is designed to be informative and personal. Whether you’re new to Jakarta or considering a change of school, we’re here to help you find the right pathway for your child—with clarity, care, and expert guidance.
